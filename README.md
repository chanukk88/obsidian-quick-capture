## 📋 What is obsidian-quick-capture?

obsidian-quick-capture is a simple app that helps you save your thoughts fast. You can write or speak notes, and it sends them straight to your daily notes in Obsidian. It runs on Windows and works with Raycast, a quick keyboard tool.

This tool is for anyone who wants to keep ideas, reminders, or quick memos organized without interrupting their workflow. It helps you capture thoughts as soon as they come.

## 🎙 How to Capture Thoughts (Written and Spoken)

### Writing a Quick Note

- Press the Raycast hotkey to open obsidian-quick-capture.
- Type your note in the text box.
- Press Enter or click the save button.
- The note will save directly into your current day’s daily note file in Obsidian. You can open Obsidian to see it immediately.

### Capturing Spoken Thoughts

- Press the Raycast hotkey.
- Click the microphone icon.
- Speak clearly into your microphone.
- When you finish, the app converts your voice to text.
- The text saves to the daily note just like a typed note.

## 📂 About Your Notes and Organization

- All notes save inside your Obsidian vault under the daily notes file named by date.
- If your daily notes are disabled in Obsidian, you need to enable them for this app to organize notes correctly.
- Notes include a timestamp so you can track when you added them.
- You can edit or move notes inside Obsidian any time, just like any other file.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

- **App does not open**: Make sure your Windows is up to date. Try restarting your device.
- **Notes not saving**: Check that the app is pointed to your correct Obsidian vault folder.
- **Voice capture fails or is inaccurate**: Check your microphone settings and language selection inside the app.
- **Raycast won’t start the extension**: Confirm Raycast is running, and the extension is enabled in its settings.

If problems persist, try reinstalling the app using the steps under Download and Install.
